About The Conference

Conference Dedication: Prof. C. K. Soh, Nanyang Technological University (Singapore)/ Southeast University (Nanjing)
Platform: MS Teams

This conference aims to provide an online platform for convergence of international researchers to deliberate on futuristic technologies in engineering which shall be instrumental in the post COVID 19 era. Original papers are invited from researches based on the themes outlined below but not limited to

• Futuristic structural health monitoring, including futuristic sensing and      futuristic SHM system design

• Futuristic Piezoelectric energy harvesting and storage solutions

• Futuristic green technologies

• Futuristic bio-mechanics and bio-medical sensing including bio-medical    structructural health monitoring

• Futuristic engineering materials

• Futuristic sustainable solutions

• Futuristic applications of artificial intelligence and machine learning for     SHM

• Futuristic materials for repair and retrofitting

• Futuristic construction materials for low carbon emissions

• SPECIAL SESSION ON META MATERIALS
